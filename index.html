It can be lack of warm water, element that is not working as it should and much more. A sewer lateral line is your home’s sewer and pipe system. This line is responsible for connecting your home to the city’s waterline. This means that your sewer lateral line brings fresh water into your home and removes waste away from your home too. These crucial functions require well-maintained pipes in order to operate. If your sewer lateral line has been cracked or damaged, it’s important to call in the professionals at Challenge Rooter right away. We’ll start by assessing the damage with our video cameras. Once we get an idea of the scope of the issue, we can offer you an assessment and lay out your options for repair and replacement.

To maintain your lawn’s appearance and make sure our worksite is safe, we offer trenchless sewer lateral repair and replacement services. This is both cost-effective and time-saving and provides excellent results as we don’t have to dig up your property but can still perform our work. If you only need a simple repair, we can insert a pipe liner or sleeve into the old pipe which allows us to create a new pipe within your pipe and repair the affected area. If you need sewer lateral replacement, we can insert a new pipe through the existing sewer line, breaking away the old line.
