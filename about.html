Celebrating fifty years of trading

Argonaut Heating Ltd, established in 1972, has been providing clients with consistently high standards of service for a range of mechanical services for fifty years.

Experienced Team of Heating & Air Conditioning Engineers

The experienced Argonaut Heating Ltd team prides itself on its ability to solve problems that others have failed to resolve and are confident of providing the most efficient and cost effective solution, no matter the size of the enquiry - from a single boiler repair to a large commercial heating and air conditioning design project.

Specialist Commercial Heating, Ventilating & Air Conditioning Contractor

We specialise in a full design, installation, repair and maintenance service for industrial and commercial heating, ventilating, plumbing and air conditioning systems for a wide range of project sizes and building types including schools, colleges, churches, embassies, private members' clubs, art galleries, offices factories and warehouses in the London, East Anglia and South East area including central London and all London boroughs, Essex, Suffolk, Norfolk, Lincolnshire, Peterborough, Cambridgeshire, Hertfordshire, Bedfordshire, Northamptonshire, Leicestershire, Middlesex, Berkshire and Buckinghamshire.
